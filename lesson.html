What is a Budget?

A budget is a plan for how you will spend and save your money. It helps you:

  • Track where your money goes
  • Make sure you have enough for important things
  • Save for future goals
  • Avoid spending more than you have
Fun Fact: Even big businesses use budgets! They call it "financial planning."

Budgeting Tips

The 50/30/20 Rule

A simple way to budget is the 50/30/20 rule:

  • 50% for Needs: Things you must have (food, clothes)
  • 30% for Wants: Things you'd like to have (toys, games)
  • 20% for Savings: Money you save for later

Smart Saving Tips

  • Set a savings goal (like a new toy or game)
  • Use jars or envelopes to separate money
  • Wait 24 hours before buying something you want
  • Look for sales and compare prices
