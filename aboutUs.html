About BizVenture Kids

Empowering young minds with practical financial education through interactive simulations and real-world business scenarios.

Our Mission
At BizVenture Kids, we believe that financial literacy is one of the most important life skills children can learn. Our mission is to make financial education engaging, practical, and fun for kids aged 8-15 through interactive business simulations.
We create safe, game-based learning environments where children can:
  • Run virtual businesses and make real financial decisions
  • Learn about revenue, expenses, profit, and loss in a risk-free setting
  • Develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Gain confidence in making financial choices
  • Understand the value of money and resources
Our simulations are designed to be age-appropriate, educational, and entertaining, helping kids develop essential money management skills that will serve them throughout their lives.
Our Story
BizVenture Kids was founded in 2020 by a team of educators, parents, and financial experts who noticed a critical gap in financial education for children. While schools teach math and basic economics, most kids graduate without understanding how to:
Learning through play
Financial literacy starts young
Real-world business experience
  • Manage money in real-life situations
  • Make informed spending decisions
  • Understand how businesses work
  • Develop entrepreneurial thinking
We set out to create a solution that would make financial education interactive, engaging, and relevant to kids' lives. Our simulations allow children to experience the thrill of running a business, the challenge of making tough financial decisions, and the satisfaction of seeing their efforts pay off - all in a safe, game-like environment.
Since our launch, we've helped thousands of children in over 20 countries develop financial confidence and business acumen. Our platform continues to grow with new simulations, learning paths, and educational resources.
